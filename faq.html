Using Any Brew™

It's incredibly simple - you don't use a whole bottle at a time:

  • Hot Coffee: Mix a portion of the bottle with hot water
  • Iced Coffee: Mix a portion of the bottle with cold water and ice
  • Latte: Mix a portion of the bottle with milk (hot or cold)
  • Cold Brew: Mix a portion of the bottle with cold water and let steep

Each bottle makes multiple perfect cups of coffee! With 2 bottles per shipment, you'll have 2 weeks of personalized coffee.

Store your Any Brew™ bottles in a cool, dry place. They don't need refrigeration and will stay fresh for up to 6 months. Keep them away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures.
