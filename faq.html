About Any Brew™

Any Brew™ is a personalized coffee extract that adapts to your taste preferences. It's designed to make any coffee drink you want - hot coffee, iced coffee, lattes, cold brew - all from one concentrated formula that's customized just for you.

Our coffee experts use your quiz responses to create a unique formula that matches your taste preferences. We consider factors like:

  • Your preferred roast level (light, medium, dark)
  • Brew method preferences
  • Flavor profile preferences (nutty, fruity, floral, smoky)
  • How you like to drink your coffee (black, with milk, etc.)
  • Your feedback on each batch

Any Brew™ is a concentrated coffee extract, not ground coffee beans. This means:

  • It's already extracted and ready to use
  • You can make any coffee drink instantly
  • No brewing equipment needed
  • Consistent taste every time
  • It gets better with your feedback

Using Any Brew™

It's incredibly simple:

  • Hot Coffee: Mix 1 bottle with hot water
  • Iced Coffee: Mix 1 bottle with cold water and ice
  • Latte: Mix 1 bottle with milk (hot or cold)
  • Cold Brew: Mix 1 bottle with cold water and let steep

Each bottle makes one perfect cup of coffee! With 2 bottles per shipment, you'll have 2 weeks of personalized coffee.

Store your Any Brew™ bottles in a cool, dry place. They don't need refrigeration and will stay fresh for up to 6 months. Keep them away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures.

Yes! You can easily adjust the strength by using more or less water. For stronger coffee, use less water. For milder coffee, use more water. The beauty of Any Brew™ is that it's completely customizable to your taste.
